VN

Vice-Principal

The school library is the heart of our learning environment. It is a space where curiosity is encouraged, ideas are explored, and a lifelong love for reading is nurtured. Our library provides students with access to a wide range of books, digital resources, and learning activities that support both academic growth and personal development.

Through reading programs, quiz competitions, storytelling sessions, and other engaging activities, the library helps students build confidence, improve communication skills, and expand their knowledge beyond the classroom. It also teaches them essential values such as responsibility, discipline, and independent learning.

I am proud of the continuous efforts made by our librarian and staff to create a vibrant and welcoming space for all learners. Together, we will continue to inspire our students to read more, learn more, and grow into well-informed and responsible individuals.

— Shri Vikram Narvekar, Principal, Sri Ranganath International School, Bagalkot

About the SRI Library

The heart of Sri Ranganath International School's academic community — your gateway to knowledge.

Overview
About Sri Ranganath International School

Sri Ranganath International School is an English medium school affiliated by the Central Board of Secondary Education (C.B.S.E), New Delhi. It is the dream child of Kavade Educational Trust, Bagalkot.

The school is co-educational and commenced its academic functions from the year July 2009–2010. The school believes in providing international quality education at affordable costs.

With a focus on developing multitasking creative team players, the management has a strategic tie-up with "Academy for Creative Teaching" (ACT) to enhance creativity, discipline, and quality systems in the institution.

At SRI, a dedicated team of teachers re-orient themselves to new ways of knowledge management with technology-based teaching with an emphasis on self-learning.

The SRI Library is a resource centre for teaching, learning and being the heart of the academic centre, it is home for all the information services. It plays a proactive role in enabling access to information resources of all kinds.

The Library holds a hybrid collection of printed as well as electronic resources which include books, journals, online books, audio-visuals, CDs/DVDs, e-books, e-journals, etc. Currently the library holds over 3000 books, newspapers and magazines.

📋 Library Rules & Guidelines
  • Students must carry their library card at all times
  • Maintain silence and discipline inside the library
  • Handle books and materials with care
  • Return borrowed books on time to avoid penalties
  • No food or drinks are allowed inside the library
  • Mobile phones must be kept on silent mode
  • Lost or damaged books must be replaced by the student
